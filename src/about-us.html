About Us

MTB Carpentry & Construction is a locally owned & operated company with over 15 years experience & expertise in the building & construction industry.

We are a fully licensed and insured builder meaning you don’t have to worry about the service, during or after the job. We have a great team of licensed tradesman working with us who are also helpful, friendly and provide a high quality of work. Any trade can be arranged to save you ringing around to find a decent tradesman that will show up, and ontime. Trades such as electricians, painters, plumbers, plasterers, concreters, air conditioning installers, kitchen manufacturers, ceramic or roof tilers and renderers are only a phone call away.

Over time we have established connections with huge list suppliers which means the price and quality of materials are always going to be the best.

How we stand out against the rest:

  • Outstanding customer care and communication
  • 15 years of experience in all aspects of carpentry and construction
  • Can undertake any carpentry or construction job regardless of the size
  • Great prices, so you don’t pay over the odds
  • Free consultations and quotations available
  • Friendly and polite staff
